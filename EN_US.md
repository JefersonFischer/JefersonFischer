### About

Senior Software Developer with 15+ years of experience, including 6+ years focused on backend development using Java and Kotlin within microservices architectures at enterprise scale. Currently working as a Specialist Developer at **Grupo Boticário**, one of Brazil's largest beauty and cosmetics groups, where I contribute to the design, development, and evolution of REST APIs and business-critical microservices.

I have solid expertise in **Apache Kafka** for distributed event-driven systems, **Spring Boot / Spring Cloud** for building resilient and observable services, and **AWS** for cloud deployment and operations. I apply **TDD**, **SOLID**, and **Clean Code** as standard engineering practices, with a consistent focus on quality, maintainability, and performance.

Prior to specializing in backend development, I worked in industrial automation (C/C++, PLCs, HMIs), which gave me a strong foundation in systems thinking, systems integration, and problem-solving in mission-critical environments.

### Key Differentiators

- Enterprise-scale experience with high-availability, high-throughput distributed systems
